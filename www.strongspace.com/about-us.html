About us

Welcome to Strongspace - the solution to your fitness struggles! We know firsthand how difficult it can be to find the right gym or fitness class in your area. Between the lack of online resources, hidden fees, and confusing schedules, it's no wonder that many people feel overwhelmed and discouraged when it comes to their fitness journey. That's where Strongspace comes in. Our subscription-based service provides a hassle-free way to access any gym or fitness class at any time, just like OpenTable. We believe that everyone should have the opportunity to reach their fitness goals, regardless of where they live or how busy their schedule may be. At Strongspace, we're dedicated to providing a seamless and stress-free experience for our customers.

Join us

With our easy-to-use platform, you can quickly and easily book the fitness experience you want, without any surprises or headaches. Plus, our friendly customer support team is always available to help you with any questions or concerns you may have. Join the Strongspace community today and take the first step towards achieving your fitness goals. We can't wait to help you discover your strong!
